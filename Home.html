Save The Children

Introduction

Save the Children is a organization that aims to protect and fight for the rights of children. The foundation ensures that a healthy start at a great education. foundation doesn’t spare any expense when it comes to the education of the kids it its care. Save the Children has done amazing work in 110 countries for over 100 years and has no plans of slowing down. Save the Children is everywhere where there are children in need. The organisation will always keep you informed on any recent news and developments, reports and any insight from the 110 countries where they operate.


We aim to help as much kids as possible no matter where they be in the world. In recent years we have made contact with kids that live in rural areas this is ground breaking for us because over the years we have found it diffecult to get in contact with kids and their parents this means we can help more kids with issues such as health care and education.
